CivicSync

CivicSync – Civic-Tech for Better Service Delivery CivicSync is a web application built to improve municipal service delivery in South African townships and rural areas. It allows residents to report local issues—like potholes or waste problems—directly to municipalities through a mobile-friendly platform. By connecting citizens, officials, and contractors, CivicSync streamlines issue resolution while promoting transparency and accountability.
